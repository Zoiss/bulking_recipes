Turkey Burger

This turkey burger recipe is a quick and provide a significant amount of protien for your needs and tastes great

Ingredients

  • Ground Turkey
  • 2 tablespoones olive oil
  • Buns
  • Seasoning and topping of choice
  1. Heat a skillet on medium heat and add olive oil
  2. Mash Ground Turkey into a cohesive patty
  3. Add patty of skillet, letting cook for 3 minutes, then flipping
  4. Ensure meat is safe to eat with a themometer
  5. Season, add toppings and enjoy!
