Spicy Lentils with Blue Corn Chips

This lentil recipe never gets old, and if you like spice, it's got plenty of it! This is the perfect post workout meal and is also a vegan recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup lentils
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 1/4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon lime juice
  • 1/2 Tablespoon garam masala
  1. Fill medium sized pot with 2 cups water and 2 cups vegetable broth
  2. Add 1 cup washed lentils
  3. Bring pot to a boil
  4. Reduce heat to simmer and cover for 20 minutes
  5. Drain Lentils, sautee in pan with sauce
